Emoji Match

Instructions:

Emoji Match is a simple memory tile game - there are 8 pairs of emojis hidden behind the tiles. Clicking on a tile will show the emoji underneath it and if the two emojis are the same they are removed from the board. To win you have to remove all of the emojis from the board - aim for the shortest time and the least amount of guesses possible!

Once the game is finished players will be given a ranking based on their performance - can you get a 5 star rank?
